Privacy Policy

Oscar's Assistant

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Oscar's Assistant ("we", "our", "us") operates a Facebook Messenger and Instagram Direct Message bot. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our bot.

2. Information We Collect

When you message our bot, we may receive:

  • Your Facebook or Instagram user ID (a page-scoped identifier)
  • Your public profile name and profile picture
  • The content of messages you send to the bot

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your messages and provide the bot's services
  • To maintain conversation context for a better experience
  • To improve and monitor the performance of our service

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your messages may be processed by third-party AI services to generate responses. We do not share your information with other third parties except as required by law.

5. Data Retention

Conversation data is retained for up to 30 days of inactivity, after which it may be deleted. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Stop interacting with the bot at any time
